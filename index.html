Biography

I am currently a 4th year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering Department, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Jiaya Jia. My research interest includes computer vision, deep learning, and medical image analysis. Recently I am focusing on semantic segmentation, 3D scene understanding and image synthesis.

Before that, I got the B.Eng degree in Electronic Science and Technology at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2014.
