Computer Vision and Machine Intelligence Lab (CVMI Lab)

Our lab is dedicated to advancing computer vision, deep learning, and artificial intelligence. Our aim is to push visual intelligence forward toward open-world environments and power its applications on open-ended agents. We believe it has the potential to transform future manufacturing, robotics, autonomous driving, virtual reality and more, thereby generate positive impacts on our lives. Currently, we are interested in exploring the following topics:

  • Open-world intelligence: Representation, representation learning, data-centric methods, efficient and trustworthiness models.
  • Interactive environment simulation: 3D reconstruction, neural rendering, 3D generation, and motion simulation.
  • Applications on open-ended agents: Vision for robotics, language as an interface for visual models.

    • We are also interested in utilizing AI to advance scientific and medical research (AI for Science and Medicine).
